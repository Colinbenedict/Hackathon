
# Vizient Style Guide for UI/UX (Hackathon App)

## Brand Colors (Hex)

- Vizient Orange: #FF4E00
- Vizient Purple: #565EAA
- Vizient Green: #93C840
- Vizient Turquoise: #018380
- Vizient Yellow: #FFC02E
- Vizient Gray (text): #444444
- Vizient White: #FFFFFF
- Vizient Black: #000000

## Primary Font

- Soleto (Sans-serif)
  - Use 'Soleto' for all headings and body copy
  - Fallback: `font-family: 'Soleto', sans-serif`

## Buttons

- Background: Vizient Purple (#565EAA)
- Text: White
- Height: 36px
- Padding: 0 16px
- Border-radius: 4px
- Font-size: 14px
- Font-weight: 500
- Hover: Slight opacity change (0.92)
- Focus: Slight opacity change (0.88)

## Inputs & Form Fields

- Text inputs:
  - Border: none
  - Border-bottom: 1px solid rgba(0,0,0,.42)
  - Focused border: 2px solid Vizient Purple
- Checkboxes:
  - Default: 24px, border #666
  - Checked: Background Vizient Purple, checkmark ✓
- Radio buttons:
  - Checked: Inner circle fill Vizient Purple

## Headings

- Use sentence case unless all caps is required
- Main headline: Soleto Medium or Bold, 28-50px
- Subhead: Soleto Regular or Italic, 16-20px

## Layout

- Use a grid layout (6 or 12 columns)
- Maintain consistent margin: 0.5in (or 40px)
- Use white space generously

## General Tone

- Clean, clear, confident
- Avoid clutter
- Prioritize user-friendly flow (sign-up form should be front and center)

## Notes

- Avoid gradients, shadows, or border outlines unless specified
- Do not use Vizient Yellow (#FFC02E) on white backgrounds
- Always use the registered Vizient® name correctly (not possessive form)
